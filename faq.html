Frequently asked Questions

Get some more clarity
  • Is AirMenus an app?

    There is no app download or any sign-up required for guests to order/view your menu. AirMenus is a web-app & works on any browser.

  • How much commission does AirMenus charge?

    ZERO! Yes, we are a zero commissions platform.

  • What is the pricing?

    Simple, fair pricing. No setup or onboarding fees. We have monthly subscription plans that start at Rs. 699 per month.

  • Do I get access to my customer data?

    Yes, you own the customer data. You can access your customer data along with insights about sales performance, conversion insights and various reports via AirMenus Partner Dashboard.

  • How do guests visit my AirMenu?

    There are two ways of accessing AirMenus:
    1. Dine-in: For table ordering, guests can simply scan a QR code placed on their table.
    2. For delivery or take-away: Share your AirMenus URL via your social media channels, websites and WhatsApp/SMS.

  • How does my guest pay?

    We work with Razorpay as our payment gateway provider. All credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI methods are accepted. All transactions are secure, and routed through Razorpay. All transactions will be credited to your RazorPay account (provided by us). There is no paper-work required from your end to set-up payments. We do everything for you.

  • How and when do I receive payments into my bank account?

    Razorpay takes care of settlements too. All transactions will be settled to your bank account within T+2 working days.

  • What are the payment gateway charges?

    RazorPay charges a payment gateway fee of 2% + taxes on each transaction. For more details you may refer their website

  • Can I disable online ordering & still use AirMenus instead of paper menus?

    Absolutely. We can disable ordering functionality and AirMenus will act like a Mobile Menu for your guests. In this case, your staff can take orders vocally.

  • Can I use AirMenus for Dine-ins only and not for Take-Away/Delivery or vice-versa?

    Yes. You may enable/disable any of the three as per your need. In case you wish to use for only Dine-ins It just takes one click to disable Take-Away/Delivery menus.

  • Can I customize my menu?

    You have complete control over your menu. Our cloud-based AirMenus Partner Dashboard tool allows you to manage your menu from anywhere. Any changes to your menu on the Partner Dashboard, reflect instantly on your menu. You can manage menus for multiple outlets from the same account.
